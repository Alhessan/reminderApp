## Full Description

**RoutineLoop** is your personal habit and reminder tracker that helps you build consistent routines and never miss a recurring task.

🎯 **SMART REMINDERS THAT WORK**
Set daily, weekly, or monthly reminders with precise notification times. Get reminded at the exact moment you need — whether it's morning workouts, weekly meetings, or monthly checkups.

📊 **CYCLE HISTORY & STREAKS**
See your complete task history at a glance. Track every completion, skip, and missed cycle with a visual timeline. Build streaks and stay motivated.

✅ **FLEXIBLE TASK MANAGEMENT**
- Create tasks with custom categories (Health & Fitness, Social, Learning, and more)
- Set frequency: daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, or one-time
- Choose notification time and method
- Pause and resume tasks anytime
- Skip a cycle without breaking your flow

🔒 **100% PRIVATE — YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE**
No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. All your data is stored locally on your phone. We cannot see your data — ever.

⚡ **WORKS OFFLINE**
No internet required. Your reminders and tasks work perfectly without connectivity.

🌙 **DARK MODE SUPPORT**
Comfortable viewing day or night with automatic dark mode support.

**Perfect for:**
- 💪 Fitness & workout routines
- 📚 Study & learning schedules
- 👥 Social activities & meetups
- 💊 Medication & health reminders
- 🧹 Household chores & cleaning schedules
- 📅 Bill payments & monthly reviews

**Key Features:**
✓ Recurring task management with flexible frequencies
✓ Visual cycle timeline with completion history
✓ Custom task categories with color coding
✓ Push notifications with exact timing
✓ Pause, resume, skip, and retroactive completion
✓ Task statistics and progress tracking
✓ 100% offline — no internet needed
✓ 100% private — no data collection
